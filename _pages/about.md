Welcome! I am a 2020 graduate of the University of Southern California, where I studied Economics and Political Science. I am currently a predoctoral research fellow at the [Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research][Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research] (SIEPR) and the Department of Economics with Professor [Matthew Gentzkow][Matthew Gentzkow]. Previously, I served as a research assistant at the [Brown University School of Public Health Incubator Lab][Brown University School of Public Health Incubator Lab] with Dean [Ashish Jha][Ashish Jha]. My research agenda focuses on topics related to health and information inequality in the U.S. and abroad. For more information on my professional background, please see my [Research][Research] page or my [CV][CV].
